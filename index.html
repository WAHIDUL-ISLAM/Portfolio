Exploratory Assignment in Guingajhuri Haor

Participated in an exploratory assignment focused on biodiversity and ecosystem dynamics in Guingajhuri Haor. This assignment involved fieldwork to assess the ecological interactions and species diversity within the haor ecosystem.

Market Channels Observation of Local Fish Markets

I observed and documented the market channels of local fish markets in Habiganj Sadar, analyzing the distribution and sales dynamics of freshwater fish. This study provided valuable insights into pricing strategies, supply chain operations, and the role of intermediaries in the local fish trade, contributing to a better understanding of market trends and economic factors influencing the fisheries sector.

Freshwater Fish Species Listing

Compiled a comprehensive list of freshwater fish species found in Bangladesh. This resource serves as a valuable reference for researchers, students, and enthusiasts interested in the rich biodiversity of Bangladesh's freshwater ecosystems.

Blood Collection from Live Fish

I practically collected blood from live fish for observation, gaining hands-on experience in biological sampling techniques that are essential for aquaculture and research on aquatic species health. This process enhanced my understanding of physiological assessments and allowed me to appreciate the importance of health monitoring in sustainable fisheries management.

Identification of Bones and Scales in Fish

Executed fish dissections to arrange and identify different bones and scales, providing insights into the skeletal structure and adaptations of fish species.

Practical Dissection of Shrimp

I conducted practical dissection of shrimp during the first semester, which enhanced my skills in anatomical exploration and deepened my understanding of crustacean biology.

Plankton Identification Study

I conducted a comprehensive study on plankton in the Khowai River, where I identified various species of phytoplankton and zooplankton. This research not only aimed to enhance my understanding of aquatic ecosystems but also explored the vital role that plankton play in freshwater environments, including their impact on nutrient cycling and food web dynamics. Through this study, I developed essential research skills, such as sample collection, microscopic analysis, and data interpretation, contributing to a broader understanding of biodiversity and the ecological health of freshwater habitats.
